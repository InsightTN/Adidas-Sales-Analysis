# Adidas-Sales-Analysis

# 📊 Operational & Revenue Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project presents a **comprehensive Operational and Revenue Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI**, designed to provide actionable insights into business performance across different sales categories and time periods.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to evaluate **revenue performance, operational margins, product category performance, and sales distribution trends** through a visually interactive interface.

To ensure data integrity and consistency, **Microsoft Excel was used for data preprocessing and cleaning**, after which the dataset was imported into **Power BI** for advanced analysis and visualization.

The primary goal of this dashboard is to transform raw business data into **strategic intelligence that supports data-driven decision-making**, enabling organizations to identify profitable categories, understand seasonal trends, and optimize operational strategies.

# 🧹 Data Cleaning and Preparation

Before visualization, the dataset was processed using **Microsoft Excel** to improve accuracy and analytical reliability.

### Data Preparation Steps

**1. Removing Duplicates**

Duplicate records were identified and removed to avoid incorrect revenue and margin calculations.

**2. Handling Missing Values**

Missing fields within the dataset were reviewed and corrected to maintain data completeness.

**3. Data Standardization**

Product categories and sales methods were standardized to ensure consistent grouping during analysis.

**4. Data Formatting**

Key columns such as:

* Revenue
* Sales Date
* Product Category
* Sales Method
* Operational Margin

were formatted correctly to ensure accurate calculations in Power BI.

**5. Structuring the Dataset**

The dataset was organized into meaningful fields such as:

* Product Category
* Sales Method
* Revenue
* Operational Margin
* Sales Date
* Weekly Sales Activity

This structured format made it easier to create **interactive visualizations within Power BI**.

# 📊 Dashboard Components

The dashboard is composed of several analytical sections designed to provide a complete overview of operational and sales performance.

---

# 1️⃣ Sales Overview

The **Sales Overview** section displays key performance indicators (KPIs) summarizing business performance.

### Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue**
* **Profit**
* **Units Sold**
* **Orders**

Each KPI includes **Month-over-Month (MoM) performance indicators**, which help monitor whether the business performance is improving or declining.

---

# 2️⃣ Sales Over Time

This visualization analyzes **monthly sales trends throughout the year**.

### Insights

* Identifies seasonal demand patterns.
* Highlights periods of high or low sales activity.
* Helps businesses forecast future demand.

Understanding these trends allows organizations to plan **inventory, marketing campaigns, and staffing requirements more effectively**.

---

# 3️⃣ Operational Margin by Product & Sales Method

This section evaluates **profitability across product categories and sales channels**.

### Metrics Analyzed

* Operational Margin
* Product Category Performance
* Sales Method Impact

### Business Value

This analysis helps businesses determine:

* Which products generate the highest profit margins
* Which sales methods are most efficient
* Where operational improvements can be made

---

# 4️⃣ Revenue Distribution Heatmap

The **Revenue Distribution Heatmap** visualizes daily sales performance across the week.

### Key Benefits

* Identifies **peak sales days**
* Detects **low-performing sales periods**
* Helps optimize **marketing promotions and staffing schedules**

The heatmap provides a clear representation of revenue concentration across time.

---

# 5️⃣ Category Distribution Analysis

This section provides a breakdown of **sales distribution across different product categories**.

### Examples of Categories

* Apparel
* Footwear
* Accessories
* Other retail products

The analysis highlights **which categories contribute most to total revenue**.

---

# 6️⃣ Category Performance Insights

The dashboard includes an **insight panel** summarizing key observations about product performance.

Examples of insights include:

* Best-performing product categories
* Categories with the highest operational margins
* Sales methods driving the most revenue

These insights provide **quick strategic interpretations for decision-makers**.

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

From the dashboard analysis, several insights can be derived:

### 1. Seasonal Sales Patterns

Sales fluctuate significantly across different months, indicating the influence of **seasonal demand patterns**.

### 2. High-Performing Product Categories

Certain categories consistently generate higher operational margins, making them key revenue drivers.

### 3. Sales Channel Efficiency

Different sales methods contribute differently to profitability, highlighting opportunities for **channel optimization**.

### 4. Weekly Sales Behavior

The heatmap reveals **specific days where revenue peaks**, enabling better operational planning.
